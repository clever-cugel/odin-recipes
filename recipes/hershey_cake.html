Hershey Box Cake

Description

This is the chocolate cake recipe on the back of the Hershey's Cocoa Powder box. Accompanying it is the recipe for icing which you will also want to prepare for this cake. You can shape it however you want, but I recommend a sheet cake.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1-3/4 cups flour
  • 3/4 cup cocoa
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup boiling water
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1-1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1-1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • Chocolate frosting

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 350f
  2. Combine dry ingredients in bowl
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, vanilla and mix
  4. Stir in boiling water
  5. Pour into pans and bake for 30-35 minutes
  6. Apply chocolate frosting
