Peanut Butter Sandwich

Description

If you're too lazy to cook at all, you can always make a peanut butter sandwich. It's literally just two pieces of bread and peanut butter slapped together.

Ingredients

  • Sandwich bread
  • Peanut butter

Steps

  1. Slather peanut butter on bread
  2. Put the second piece on the first
  3. That's it
