Egg Fried Rice

Description

This is a simple meal you can prepare when you're feeling lazy but not so lazy that you can't cook.Expect a savory dish with a lot of sodium.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 50g rice
  • A lot of soy sauce
  • 1 tsp Oil

Steps

  1. Prepare rice
  2. Oil pan
  3. Set stove to medium heat
  4. Add eggs and rice to pan
  5. Douse in soy sauce
  6. Scramble until it seems cooked
  7. Enjoy
